Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 16, 2026

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
We stand behind the Declutter to Cash Challenge with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you complete all 5 days and cannot identify at least $100 worth of items to sell, we'll refund your purchase.

Our Guarantee

We're confident that the Declutter to Cash Challenge will help you find valuable items in your home. That's why we offer a straightforward 30-day money-back guarantee.

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a refund, you must:

  1. Request the refund within 30 days of your purchase date
  2. Complete all 5 days of the Challenge
  3. Apply the strategies taught in the Challenge
  4. Be unable to identify at least $100 worth of sellable items in your home

What Constitutes Completion?

Challenge completion means you have:

  • Accessed and reviewed all 5 daily modules
  • Completed the assessment exercises for each day
  • Made a good-faith effort to identify items as instructed
  • Documented your progress (photos or lists of items reviewed)

How to Request a Refund

If you meet the eligibility requirements, follow these steps:

  1. Email us at hello@itemvalue.app within 30 days of purchase
  2. Include your order number and purchase date
  3. Provide brief documentation showing your completion of all 5 days
  4. Explain that you were unable to find $100 worth of items despite following the program

Processing Time

Once we receive your refund request with the required documentation:

  • We will review your request within 3 business days
  • If approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Funds will be returned to your original payment method
  • You may see the refund on your statement within 7-10 business days, depending on your bank

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided if:

  • You request a refund after 30 days from purchase
  • You have not completed all 5 days of the Challenge
  • You have identified $100+ worth of items but choose not to sell them
  • You have violated our Terms & Conditions
  • You have shared or distributed Challenge materials without permission

Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds. The Challenge is sold as a complete program, and all materials are made available immediately upon purchase.

Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties accessing the Challenge materials, please contact us immediately at hello@itemvalue.app. We will work to resolve any technical issues promptly. Technical issues that prevent access may extend your refund eligibility period.

Questions or Concerns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance with the Challenge, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Email: hello@itemvalue.app
Response time: Within 24 hours on business days

Our Commitment
We're committed to your success. Before requesting a refund, please reach out to our support team. We're here to help you get the most value from the Challenge and overcome any obstacles you might face.
